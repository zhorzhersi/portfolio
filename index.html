About ME

WHo AM i

Hey there! I'm an enthusiastic graphic designer, videographer, photographer, and motion artist with 26 years of age.

As long as I remember I was creating or building something from scratch.
As a young fifteen-year-old teenager I spontaneously fall in love with the Photoshop program. I was just googling "How to make harry potter patronus in photography..." Little did I know, that moment will changed my whole life. I started to go and look at creative art for hours a day after school. I don't even know how many hours of tutorials did I look, but that habit stayed with me. Since I got more into the creative industry, I uncovered the fantastic world of content creators and designers videographers... I have so much more passions I would like to talk about...

Now am working as a designer, and I'm all about creating awesome experiences for users and potential customers. My ultimate goal? Craft visuals that not only look stunning but also make your customers' lives easier and more enjoyable. "It’s very easy to be different, but very difficult to be better.” ad Jony Ive used to say.

Thanks for checking out my portfolio. Let's do this!
